OUR BELOVED UNIVERSITY

UNIVERSITY OF THE PHILIPPINES VISAYAS - MIAGAO CAMPUS
              The University of the Philippines Visayas - Miagao Campus is the flagship campus of UPV, specializing in fisheries,
marinesciences,  and liberal arts.  It houses the  College of Fisheries and Ocean Sciences (CFOS) and the College of Arts and
Sciences(CAS), Fisheries and Ocean Sciences(CFOS) and the College of Arts and Sciences (CAS), offering degree programs
in the sciences,humanities, and social sciences. The campus is known for its strong research focus, particularly in aquatic and
environmental studies,  contributing to national  and global discussions on marine  biodiversity and  sustainable  fisheries.  Its
strategic location along the coast provides students with direct access to marine  ecosystems,  making it a  premier  institution
for fisheries and ocean sciences in the Philippines.
